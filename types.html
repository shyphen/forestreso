Types Of Forest Resources

Renewable Recources
Renewable resources are natural elements replenished over time through natural processes, such as sunlight, wind, and water. They are sustainable alternatives to finite resources, offering continuous availability for human use without depletion. Harnessing renewable resources promotes environmental stewardship and reduces dependence on fossil fuels.
Non-Renewable Resources
Non-renewable resources are finite substances formed over geological time scales, such as fossil fuels (coal, oil, natural gas) and minerals (like metals and ores), which cannot be replenished within a human lifespan. Their extraction and consumption lead to depletion, making conservation and efficient use imperative for sustainable development.
